💧 Leak Types

Types of Pipe Leaks We Repair in Austin, TX

Every pipe leak is different. We handle all types — from hairline cracks to catastrophic bursts — across all pipe materials found in Austin homes.

💥

Burst Pipe Repair

Austin's winter freeze events (February 2021 was devastating) cause pipes to freeze and burst, releasing hundreds of gallons per minute. We respond immediately to isolate the break, shut off water, and perform emergency repairs to prevent catastrophic flood damage.

🔍

Pinhole & Hidden Leaks

Older copper pipes in Austin's Hyde Park, Rosedale, and Crestview neighborhoods are prone to pinhole leaks caused by aggressive water chemistry. Our electronic acoustic detection equipment finds pinhole leaks inside walls and under floors without unnecessary demolition.

🏠

Slab Leak Repair

Pipes embedded in your concrete slab can develop leaks due to Austin's expansive clay soil movement. We use ground microphones and thermal imaging to pinpoint slab leaks to within inches — then use minimally invasive tunneling or slab access to make targeted repairs.

🚿

Supply Line Leaks

Braided supply lines to toilets, sinks, and appliances fail without warning. A burst washing machine or dishwasher supply line can dump hundreds of gallons of water in minutes. We replace all supply lines with braided stainless steel for durability.

🔄

Repiping Services

Galvanized steel pipes in Austin's older homes corrode from the inside out, restricting flow and leaking at joints. When repairs are no longer cost-effective, we perform full or partial repiping in PEX — the modern standard for residential plumbing.

🌡️

Thermal Expansion Leaks

Austin's extreme temperature swings — from summer highs above 110°F to winter freezes — cause pipes to expand and contract, stressing joints and connections. We identify and reinforce vulnerable connections to prevent seasonal leaks before they start.

⚡ Our Process

How We Find & Fix Pipe Leaks in Austin

We use a systematic, technology-driven approach to locate leaks accurately and repair them efficiently — minimizing damage and disruption to your Austin home.

🔍

Electronic Detection

We use acoustic listening devices and thermal imaging cameras to detect leaks behind walls, under floors, and in concrete slabs — without pulling down drywall blindly.

📋

Accurate Diagnosis

We pinpoint the exact leak location to within inches. This means we only open the wall or slab exactly where needed — minimizing repair footprint and restoration costs.

🔧

Targeted Repair

Using the right materials for your pipe type — copper, PEX, CPVC, galvanized — we perform a precise, code-compliant repair with OEM-quality parts and our 1-year warranty.

✅

Pressure Test & Sign-Off

After every repair, we pressure-test the line to confirm zero leakage before closing the access point. We document the repair with photos for your records and any insurance claim.
