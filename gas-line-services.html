If You Smell Gas in Austin — Do This First

🚨 Gas Leak Emergency Protocol

Step 1: Stop what you're doing — do not flip any light switches, use lighters, or operate any electrical devices.
Step 2: Get everyone out of the building immediately — leave doors open as you exit.
Step 3: Move at least 300 feet away from the building.
Step 4: Call 911 from outside — then call Atmos Energy at 1-800-460-3030 to shut off service.
Step 5: Call us at (512) 277-5691 — our certified gas line technicians will respond immediately to locate and seal the leak.

🔧 Gas Line Services

Gas Line Services We Provide in Austin, TX

Every gas line service we perform meets City of Austin code requirements and Atmos Energy standards. Permits handled. Inspections passed. Every time.

🔍

Gas Leak Detection

We use calibrated gas detection equipment to identify leaks in your gas system — from the meter to individual appliances. Electronic sensors detect even trace amounts of gas that your nose might not catch, ensuring complete safety before re-occupancy.

🔧

Gas Line Repair

Corroded fittings, cracked flexible connectors, deteriorated flex lines, and compromised joints are all repaired to current Austin code. We perform pressure testing after every repair to guarantee a 100% gas-tight system before we leave.

🏗️

New Gas Line Installation

Adding a gas range, outdoor grill, fireplace, generator, or whole-home generator? We design and install new gas lines from your meter to the appliance — with proper sizing for flow rate and pressure, and all required permits and inspections.

🔄

Gas Line Replacement

Older black iron or galvanized gas lines in Austin homes corrode over time. We replace outdated systems with modern CSST (corrugated stainless steel tubing) or Schedule 40 black iron pipe — properly bonded and grounded per Austin code.

🍳

Appliance Gas Hookup

New gas range, dryer, water heater, or outdoor kitchen? We install new gas connections with proper flexible connectors, shutoff valves, and pressure testing. We also certify connections so your appliance warranty remains valid.

📋

Gas System Inspection & Testing

Before buying a home or after any major renovation, a full gas system pressure test ensures your Austin home's gas lines are safe and code-compliant. We test all lines, document the results, and provide a written safety report.
