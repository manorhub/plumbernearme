⚠️ Warning Signs

Signs Your Austin Water Heater Needs Immediate Service

Don't wait until your water heater fails completely — early warning signs can save you thousands in water damage and emergency replacement costs.

🌡️

No Hot Water or Lukewarm Water

The most obvious sign. Could be a failed heating element, tripped breaker, bad thermostat, or pilot light issue — all fixable same day. Don't shower cold when a licensed plumber is one call away.

💧

Pooling Water Around the Tank

Water on the floor around your water heater is a serious warning. Could be a failing pressure relief valve, loose connection, or cracked tank. A cracked tank cannot be repaired — call immediately to prevent flooding.

🟤

Rusty or Discolored Water

Reddish-brown hot water means your tank is corroding from the inside. This is a sign the unit is near the end of its lifespan. The anode rod may also be depleted — call for a diagnosis before the tank fails completely.

🔊

Popping, Rumbling, or Banging Noises

Loud noises from your water heater are caused by sediment buildup — hardened deposits settling on the heating element. Austin's hard water makes this accelerated. Annual flushing prevents this and extends tank life significantly.
