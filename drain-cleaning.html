⚠️ Warning Signs

Signs You Need Drain Cleaning Now

Most Austin homeowners wait too long to call a plumber. These warning signs mean your drains need professional attention before a minor clog becomes a major backup.

⚠️

Slow-Draining Fixtures

Water pooling in your sink, shower, or tub that drains slowly is the earliest warning sign. A partial blockage is much easier and cheaper to fix than a complete stoppage.

🔊

Gurgling & Bubbling Sounds

Gurgling from your drains when water drains elsewhere means air is trapped by a blockage. This is an early indicator of a main line issue — call before it backs up completely.

👃

Sewage or Rotten Odors

Persistent foul smells from drains indicate organic buildup, trapped debris, or a dry P-trap. Left untreated, this can mean bacteria growth and serious blockage development.

🔄

Recurring Clogs

If the same drain keeps clogging every few weeks, the blockage was never fully cleared. Professional hydro-jetting removes the root cause rather than just pushing the clog down the line.
