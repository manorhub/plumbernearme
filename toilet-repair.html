🔧 Toilet Services

Professional Toilet Services We Offer in Austin, TX

We repair all major toilet brands and solve everything from simple leaks to main sewer blockage backup issues.

🔧

Toilet Repair & Rebuilds

We replace worn-out fill valves, flush valves, flappers, handles, tank-to-bowl gaskets, and bolts. If your toilet is constantly running, making noise, or slow to fill, we rebuild the tank mechanics to brand-new standards.

🚽

New Toilet Installation

Upgrade to a modern, water-efficient, ADA-compliant comfort-height toilet. We install all major brands (Kohler, American Standard, Toto, Mansfield) and take care of the heavy lifting, disposal of your old toilet, and clean-up.

🌊

Clog Clearing & Augering

Plunging not working? Foreign object flushed? We use specialized toilet augers to clear stubborn traps. If the clog is further down the sewer line, we use main line snaking to restore full function.

💧

Base Leak & Wax Ring Replacement

If water is pooling around the floor base of your toilet, the wax seal has failed. We pull the toilet, inspect the flange, install a premium wax ring, reseat the toilet, and test for a perfect seal.

⚙️

Flange Repair & Replacement

A loose, wobbly, or leaking toilet is often caused by a cracked, rusted, or improperly set floor flange. We repair broken flanges with metal reinforcement rings or replace the entire flange to secure the toilet safely.

🌿

Eco-Friendly Upgrades

Austin water rates are high. Upgrading to a certified WaterSense dual-flush toilet can save a household up to 13,000 gallons of water per year. We help you choose the right high-efficiency model for your budget.
